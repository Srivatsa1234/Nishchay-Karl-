Kairali Ayurvedic Health Resort


One of the best resort Ayurveda reasort in Palakkad district of Kerala, Kairali Ayurvedic health resort is set amidst 50 acres of lush green land of Kerala.There is also a river stream that passes through each cottage. The food served here is strictly vegetarian, and alcohol is prohibited. Guests are also encouraged to participate in the early morning meditation and Yoga sessions.






















Beach and Lake Ayurvedic Resort



Private and intimate Ayurvedic resort of Kerala,Beach and Lake Resort is located 8 kilometers south of Trivandrum International Airport. It is nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Karamana River, situated on an island. It comprises of only 8 rooms with backwaters on one side and beach on the other. All the rooms of Beach and Lake Resort offer panoramic view of the surrounding areas.





















Sarovaram Ayurvedic Resort



A family owned Ayurvedic health resort of Kerala ,Sarovaram resort run and managed by the third generation of Ayurvedic doctors and well-staffed with massage experts. That is why Sarovaram resort is best for those who are on a tight budget. It is a simple place that’s spread over 3 acres, right on the water’s edge of Kollam district of Kerala. Here you can enjoy various activities like houseboat stay and visit to the nearby villages in evening even dance performances also.
